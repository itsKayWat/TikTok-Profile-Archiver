Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install the software?

Download the software from your purchase email, extract the ZIP file, and run the installer. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

How do I activate my license?

Enter your license key when prompted during the first launch of the application. You can find your license key in your purchase confirmation email.

Can I transfer my license to another computer?

Yes, you can deactivate your license on one computer and activate it on another. Contact support if you need assistance with this process.

What should I do if the software isn't working?

First, ensure you're running the latest version. If the issue persists, check our troubleshooting guide or contact support through Discord or the ticket system.
